Refund Policy

Effective date: February 22, 2026  ·  Last updated: February 22, 2026
We want you to be satisfied with DriveGuard Pro. Please read this policy carefully to understand your refund options before making a purchase.

1. Payment Processing

All purchases of DriveGuard Pro are processed by Paddle (paddle.com), our authorised reseller and Merchant of Record. Refunds are issued through Paddle back to your original payment method.

2. Standalone (Perpetual) License Refunds

Standalone licenses are one-time purchases. The following conditions apply:

  • Refund requests submitted within 14 days of the original purchase date will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
  • Refunds are not issued once a license key has been activated on a machine.
  • Refunds are not issued for change-of-mind requests after the 14-day window.
  • If the Software fails to function as described and the issue cannot be resolved by our support team, a refund may be granted at our discretion regardless of activation status.

3. Subscription Refunds

DriveGuard Pro subscriptions (monthly or annual) are billed on a recurring basis through Paddle.

  • Cancellations: You may cancel your subscription at any time via the Paddle customer portal. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period and no further charges will be made.
  • Partial refunds: We do not issue partial refunds for unused time in a billing period after cancellation.
  • Annual subscriptions: Refund requests for annual plans submitted within 14 days of the initial purchase or renewal date will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis, provided the Software has not been actively used during that period.
  • Duplicate charges: If you were charged more than once due to a billing error, contact us immediately and we will arrange a full refund of the duplicate charge.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • License keys that have been activated and used.
  • Refund requests made after the 14-day window for standalone purchases.
  • Subscription periods already consumed prior to cancellation.
  • Purchases made through unauthorised third-party resellers.
  • Accounts suspended or terminated due to violation of our Terms and Conditions.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at services@guardlinkx.com with the following information:

  • Your full name and the email address used at purchase.
  • Your Paddle order ID or transaction reference.
  • The reason for your refund request.

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 3 business days. Approved refunds are typically processed by Paddle within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment method and bank.

6. Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card provider. Unauthorised chargebacks may result in immediate suspension of your license. We are happy to resolve any billing issues directly and promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase will apply to that transaction.
