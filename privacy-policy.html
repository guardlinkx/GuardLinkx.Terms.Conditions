Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 22, 2026  ·  Last updated: February 22, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We never sell your personal data.

1. Who We Are

GuardLinkx ("we", "us", "our") is the developer and publisher of DriveGuard Pro. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at services@guardlinkx.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • Account & Purchase Information: Your name and email address collected at the time of purchase, processed and stored by our payment partner Paddle.
  • License Information: A unique license key tied to your purchase, used to activate and validate the Software on your machine.
  • Backup Data: Files you choose to back up via the cloud backup feature. This data is stored encrypted in Amazon Web Services (AWS) S3 and is accessible only to you. We do not access or read your backup data.
  • Device Information: A hardware identifier used solely to enforce single-machine activation limits. This identifier is not shared with third parties.
  • Support Communications: Any information you voluntarily provide when contacting our support team by email.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver, activate, and validate your license.
  • Provide cloud backup services to subscription users.
  • Process payments and manage billing through Paddle.
  • Respond to support inquiries and refund requests.
  • Send important product notices (e.g., license expiry, material policy updates).

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We work with a limited number of trusted third parties to operate our services:

  • Paddle (paddle.com): Our authorised reseller and Merchant of Record. Paddle handles all payment processing and stores your billing information under their Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Cloud infrastructure used to store encrypted backup data for subscription users. AWS operates under its own Privacy Notice.

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties except as required by law.

5. Data Security

We take security seriously and apply the following measures to protect your data:

  • All backup data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using AES-256 on AWS.
  • Each user's backup data is isolated under a unique, scoped access key.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel only.

While we employ industry-standard safeguards, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your license key and account credentials confidential.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law:

  • License & account data: Retained for the duration of your license plus a reasonable period for billing and legal compliance (typically 7 years).
  • Cloud backup data: Retained while your subscription is active. Upon subscription cancellation or expiry, your backup data may be deleted after a 30-day grace period.
  • Support communications: Retained for up to 2 years after the last correspondence.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data.
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at services@guardlinkx.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

The DriveGuard Pro desktop application does not use cookies. Our website may use essential cookies to maintain session state. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

DriveGuard Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice within the Software. The updated policy will be posted at this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the Software after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
